About AdiKar

AdiKar Business Consultants is a management consulting proprietorship, owned by Ms. Karishma Valliappan. Adikar was incorporated on 2nd March 2017. We provide a range of business services like HR, IT, Finance, Client Management, Social Media and Content writing to corporates as well as dance enterprises and photography studios. We also conduct workshops on various functional and creative areas like dance, photography, art and sports and events for corporates, NGO’s and Schools. The aim of AdiKar Business Consultants is to offer a whole package of services for you to pick and choose, while we let you focus on your strength.

About Karishma

Born in Malaysia and brought up in India, Karishma has worked with people from various cultural backgrounds. She has completed her Bachelor in Arts (Psychology) from Symbiosis College of Arts and Commerce. Karishma is a professional dancer and also a HR and Marketing professional. HR, Marketing, Social Media and Dance are her expertise. She has also worked as an accountant in Singapore. Coming from a psychology background Karishma has also volunteered with NGO’s such as SAA (Schizophrenia Awareness Association) and The Red Door. The idea behind having AdiKar Business Consultants is to use all her expertise and support corporates, dance companies and aspiring artists in the field of dance, photography and art.
